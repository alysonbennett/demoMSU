How To Make A Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Get a plate, knife, two slices of bread, peanut butter, and jelly
  2. Place bread slices on plate
  3. Use knife to spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread
  4. Use knife to spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread
  5. Put the two pieces of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly joining in the middle
  6. Enjoy!
